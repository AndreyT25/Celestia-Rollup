Guess The Number

This game is based on Celestia Rollkit on Ethermint and is called "GuessTheNumber". This is a lottery where you can win Ether by betting and guessing numbers.

Below are instructions on how to play this game:

  1. Installing an Ethereum wallet: Before you can play, you need to install an Ethereum wallet such as MetaMask. This will allow you to interact with the Ethereum blockchain and play the game.
  2. Wallet Settings: Add a new network. You can see how to do it in more detail "HERE"

    3. Network name: Ethermint

    New RPC URL: http://YOUR URL:8545/

    Chain ID: 9000

    Currency symbol: photon

  3. Connect to the game: After installing the wallet, you must connect to the game by clicking the "connectWallet" button. This requires your permission to access your Ethereum account.
  4. Receiving free tokens: You can receive free tokens by entering your Ethereum address in the "Enter the address to receive tokens" field and clicking the "claimFreeTokens" button. tokens will be sent to your Ethereum account.
  5. Start the game: To start the game, you need to enter the amount of the bet and three numbers from 1 to 10 in the corresponding fields (bet amount, guess1, guess2, guess3) and press the "startGame" button. This action will send the transaction to the Ethereum blockchain and start the game.
  6. Guess the number: You win the game if you guess the number randomly generated by the smart contract. You will receive the message "You won!" and double the winnings from your bet will be sent to your Ethereum account. If you did not guess the number, you will get the message "You lost!" and the bet will be forfeited.
